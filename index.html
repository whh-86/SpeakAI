About SpeakAI

SpeakAI is a real-time conversational AI system designed to help users improve their English speaking skills through natural dialogue and instant grammar feedback.

Built for ECE 6098 Speech AI, the system integrates state-of-the-art Automatic Speech Recognition (ASR), a Large Language Model (LLM), and Text-to-Speech (TTS) synthesis into one seamless pipeline.

Whisper ASR Gemini 2.5 Flash edge-tts Flask

Key Features

Real-time Grammar Correction — After each turn, the AI highlights errors with explanations and correct alternatives, helping you learn from every sentence you speak.


Adaptive Difficulty — The system automatically assesses your English level (A2 / B1 / B2) from your first few sentences and adjusts vocabulary and complexity to match your ability.


Error Tracking — Every grammar mistake is logged and categorized by type (tense, preposition, article, etc.) so you can see your patterns over time.

ASR — Speech Recognition

Your voice is captured by the microphone and processed by OpenAI Whisper, an open-source automatic speech recognition model running locally on the device.


Whisper converts your spoken English into text with high accuracy. We use the small model variant, which balances recognition quality and processing speed for real-time conversation. The transcribed text is then passed to the language model for understanding and response generation.

LLM — Language Understanding

The transcribed text is sent to Google Gemini 2.5 Flash via the Google AI Studio API. A carefully engineered system prompt instructs the model to act as an English conversation coach.


The model returns a structured JSON response containing both the conversational reply and a grammar analysis of the user's input — including identified errors, corrected forms, and explanations. The dual-output design is the core technical innovation of this system.

TTS — Speech Synthesis

The AI's reply text is converted to natural-sounding speech using edge-tts, a Python library that interfaces with Microsoft Edge's high-quality voice engine.


The synthesized audio is streamed back and played through your speakers in real time. We selected an American English voice optimized for clarity and naturalness, making it ideal for language learning. The end-to-end pipeline — from your speech to the AI's voice response — typically completes in under 3 seconds.
